🔎 Find your Gemini key — step by step
  1. Is link ko kholo: aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Apne Google account se Sign in karo.
  3. Blue button “Create API key” pe click karo.
  4. Agar puche to “Create in new project” select karo.
  5. Key ban jayegi — uske aage Copy icon dabao.
  6. Wapas yahan aake upar wale box me paste karke Save dabao. Ho gaya! ✅
